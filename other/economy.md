# GDP
Roughly speaking, GDP of a country means the income of a country. Means the money it earns without minus outcomes. Like Salary of a person. It's not what I have after spending salary, but only salary

# market share, market cap
Everything can be valued by money. Person, House, car, dog, cat, anything. When people in market "believe" a thing(including human being) worthy a value of money, then we say that he has a market cap of that value.

You can see marketcap in companiesmarketcap.com, every company has value of money. Roughly speaking it means the company owns that much of money. In companiesmarketcap.com, you can only see the companies that have already in the market, many private or government hold business arn't in the list.
